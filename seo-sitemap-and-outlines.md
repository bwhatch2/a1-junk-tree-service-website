## 3. Internal Linking Strategy

The internal linking strategy is built around **topical clusters** with the homepage as the authority hub.

### Link Architecture

**Homepage** links to:
- /services (Junk Removal hub)
- /tree-service (Tree Service hub)
- /free-estimate (primary CTA)
- /reviews
- /service-areas
- All 15 service area pages

**Services Hub (/services)** links to:
- All 63 individual junk removal service pages
- /free-estimate
- /tree-service (cross-link)

**Tree Service Hub (/tree-service)** links to:
- All 7 individual tree service pages
- /free-estimate
- /services (cross-link)

**Each Individual Service Page** links to:
- Parent hub (/services or /tree-service) via breadcrumb
- 3-4 related services (contextual sidebar links)
- All 15 service area pages
- /free-estimate (sidebar CTA)
- Phone number CTA

**Each Service Area Page** links to:
- All service hubs (/services, /tree-service)
- Top services available in that area
- /free-estimate
- Other nearby service areas

### Cross-Linking Rules
1. Every page links to /free-estimate at least once
2. Every page includes the phone number as a clickable link
3. Related services are contextually linked (not random)
4. Breadcrumbs provide hierarchical navigation on every page
5. Footer provides site-wide links to all major sections
